University of Memphis Campus EV Ride Event

Join us for an unforgettable experience!

Event Details

Date: April 15, 2025

Time: 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM

Location: Student Plaza, Near University Center (UofM)

View Location on Google Maps

The event will bring together students, staff, and faculty for hands-on EV experiences and expert discussions.

Activities

EV Test Rides

Attendees can ride along with experienced EV drivers (from dealerships or owners) to get a feel for electric vehicle performance and comfort.

EV Test Drives

Licensed students, faculty, and staff may have the opportunity to test drive EVs in a controlled, safe area (subject to approval and waiver signing).

Educational Outreach

Distribution of flyers or brochures about EV benefits, environmental impact, maintenance savings, and available incentives.

Possibly short talks or Q&A sessions with EV experts.

Information Booths & Displays

Local dealerships and EV owners will set up booths under tents to showcase different EV models.

Representatives will answer questions, demonstrate features, and share real-world ownership experiences.
